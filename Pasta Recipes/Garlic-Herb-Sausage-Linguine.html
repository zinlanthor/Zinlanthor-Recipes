Zinlanthor's Garlic & Herb Sausage Linguine

v1.7 | Updated: 2026-01-18

CRITICAL: Control heat during the Liaison step to avoid scrambled eggs.

Description: This is a savory, "limited-ingredient" pantry staple designed for efficiency. It uses a technical Roux base to create a creamy texture without requiring heavy cream, finished with a Liaison of egg yolks for a glossy, professional-grade coating.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (454g) Linguine pasta
  • 1/4 to 1/2 lb (113g-226g) Ground Breakfast Sausage
  • 4 tbsp (56g) Butter
  • 3 tbsp (24g) All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 cup (236ml) Cold Water or Milk
  • 1 cup (236ml) Hot Starchy Pasta Water (Reserved)
  • 2 tsp (10ml) Soy Sauce (or Worcestershire)
  • 2 tsp (10ml) Pickle Juice (or ACV/Lemon)
  • 2 Egg Yolks (Separated)
  • 1 tbsp (7g) Mrs. Dash Garlic & Herb seasoning
  • 2 tsp (5g) Mrs. Dash Onion & Herb seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp (0.5g) Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1/8 tsp (0.25g) Ground Nutmeg
  • 1 tsp (2g) Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 cup (113g) shredded Colby Jack cheese

Instructions

  1. Brown the sausage: Cook in a large skillet until browned. Remove meat but keep the fat. (Timer: 7 mins)
  2. Create the Roux: Add butter to fat. Whisk in flour and spices for 1 minute until a paste forms. Remove from heat.
  3. Boil the pasta: Cook linguine. Pull 3 minutes BEFORE 'Al Dente'. Reserve 1.5 cups water before draining. (Timer: 9 mins)
  4. Build the Sauce: Whisk cold liquid and seasonings into the roux until smooth. Return to low heat.
  5. The Merge: Add pasta and sausage to skillet with 1 cup pasta water. Toss over medium heat for 2-3 mins.
  6. The Liaison: Turn burner to LOWEST. Whisk pickle juice into yolks. Pour over pasta with some cheese and stir vigorously.
  7. Serving: Plate immediately and top with remaining cheese.
