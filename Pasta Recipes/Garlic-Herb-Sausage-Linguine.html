Zinlanthor's Garlic & Herb Sausage Linguine

v1.7 | Updated: 2026-01-18

CRITICAL: Turn heat to LOWEST before adding egg yolks to prevent scrambling.

Description: A savory, limited-ingredient pantry staple. This recipe uses a technical Roux base and a Liaison finish for a glossy, professional-grade coating.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (454g) Linguine pasta
  • 1/4 to 1/2 lb Ground Breakfast Sausage
  • 4 tbsp Butter / 3 tbsp Flour
  • 1 cup Cold Liquid / 1 cup Reserved Pasta Water
  • 2 Egg Yolks + 2 tsp Pickle Juice
  • Mrs. Dash Garlic & Herb and Onion & Herb seasonings
  • 1 cup shredded Colby Jack cheese

Instructions

  1. Sausage: Brown in a skillet. Remove meat, save the fat.
  2. Roux: Whisk butter, flour, and spices into fat (1 min).
  3. Pasta: Boil linguine; reserve 1.5 cups water before draining.
  4. Sauce: Whisk cold liquid into roux until smooth over low heat.
  5. Merge: Toss pasta/sausage with 1 cup pasta water in the skillet.
  6. Finish: Heat to LOWEST. Whisk yolks into juice, pour over pasta, and stir vigorously until glossy.
